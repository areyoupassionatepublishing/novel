Dan's relationship with Yulia started out as an adventurous affair that would allow him to overcome his grief after losing his wife. For Yulia, she was looking for a temporary escape from her routine life and violent husband. What transpired was a longer, more intense relationship filled with desperation and love, as they both sought to find themselves in each other's flesh. The question for Dan is whether or not he will be able to leave his guilt behind him and embrace a future with this adventurous older women. And for Yulia, whether or not she will be bold and courageous enough to leave her husband. The only thing that is certain between them, however, is their intent to find an answer to the question they discussed online. That is, their question about passion, and whether or not there is a limit to it...
